  • NEHRU MUSEUM

    The Nehru Museum building of IIT KGP was constructed about a century ago!! It is located at the Hijli Detention camp building, now named as Hijli Saheed Bhavan and is of great significance in the history of IIT KGP. It now consists of some historic traces of the old times which currently has become a good tourist attraction.
